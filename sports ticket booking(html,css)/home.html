Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and PayPal.
Can I get a refund if I can't attend the event?
Refund policies vary depending on the event and venue. Please check the specific event details on the booking page for refund information. Generally, refunds are not provided for missed events, but exchanges might be possible in certain situations.
How do I receive my tickets?
Tickets are typically delivered electronically via email as e-tickets. You can print them or show them on your mobile device at the venue. Some events may offer physical tickets, which will be shipped to your provided address.
Is there a limit to how many tickets I can purchase?
Yes, there may be a limit on the number of tickets you can purchase per transaction, depending on the event and venue. This information will be displayed during the booking process.
What should I do if I have issues with my tickets?
If you have any issues with your tickets, please contact our customer support team immediately. You can find our contact information on the "Contact" page of our website.
